Who are we?

Build the future that we envision.

At Arical, we provide our clients with the actionable location-based recommendations with the aim to improve community livability and achieve sustainable urban development. Our team consist of passionate AI and technology enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds, who are willing to contribute to the sustainable future through data-driving geospatial insights.

We have started our company with the vision that with technology, human potential becomes infinite and through years we have grown and changed a lot. We have built a product which earned the trust of many investors and today, we are proud to be accepted into and supported by HKAI Lab, AppWorks Taiwan and AWS Taiwan incubators. Everyday we continue to improve our product and build the future that we envision.

Our story

Accelerate decision-making and deal-closing processes

Arical is a startup company founded in 2018 by Clement Tien and San Wong. Both founders have had extensive experience of working in the industry before starting Arical.

Clement devotes himself to improving the quality of people’s lives through design and technology. He was responsible for the architectural design and project management of real estate developments in the United States and Hong Kong.

San is robotics and AI enthusiast striving to create innovative technological solutions for today’s world. He was actively involved in robotics, AI and computer vision projects and is the first Boston Dynamics Spot developer in Hong Kong. Both Clement and San hold degrees from leading US universities.

Our team consists of data scientists and data engineers with backgrounds in game development, astrophysics, and fintech.
